What is the relationship between diagrams and meaning?

The relationship between diagrams and meaning is a secretly complex one. Most people skip over the idea of a sign or diagram and think strictly about the meaning; Diagrams are what conveys meaning. In my personal opinion I think diagrams, symbols, and signs all fit into the category of trying to serve a purpose for some other direction, emotion, or object. When we see a photograph as I recall reading in 201, we do not think in our heads That is a photograph of a cat, we simply think That is a cat. The image serves strictly as a middle ground to convey the meaning or context. The medium (photographs, computer and paper diagrams, etc.) literally serve as a medium between object and understanding. Our bodies as a diagram, made up of individual parts that people can see as fat, skinny, ugly, pretty, etc. convey meanings to others.
